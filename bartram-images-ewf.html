Images · William Bartram’s Herbarium Specimens · East and West Florida

Folio · Botanica Caroliniana

Amy Hackney Blackwell, Christopher W. Blackwell. Botanica Caroliniana: Collaborative Research in the Liberal Arts. Furman University Department of Classics. Revision Date: 05-06-2012.

The images from William Bartram’s Hortus Siccus, East and West Florida, were photographed in November of 2011 at the Sloane Herbarium of the Natural History Museum, London, with the kind coopratation and valuable collaboration of Mark Spencer, Curator.

All content on this site is licensed under a Creative Commons License (http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/). Full- and reduced-resolution images are available for download here. Thanks to the University of Houston’s Center for High Performance Computing, Keith Crabb, Director, for hosting this data.
