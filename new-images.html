New Images of Botanical Specimens from the Jardin des Plantes and the Sloane Herbarium

Folio · Botanica Caroliniana

Amy Hackney Blackwell, Christopher W. Blackwell. Botanica Caroliniana: Collaborative Research in the Liberal Arts. Furman University Department of Classics. Revision Date: 06-13-2013.

The images of selected specimens collected by Michaux were photographed at the Jardin des Plantes, Paris, in May of 2013. The remaining images were photographed at the Sloane Herbarium, in the Natural History Museum, London, in June of 2013. The specimens on these images await further documentation.

All content on this site is licensed under a Creative Commons License (http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/). Full- and reduced-resolution images are available for download here. Thanks to the University of Houston’s Center for High Performance Computing, Keith Crabb, Director, for hosting this data.
