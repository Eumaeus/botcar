New Images of Botanical Specimens from the Sloane Herbarium: Franklin

Folio · Botanica Caroliniana

Amy Hackney Blackwell, Christopher W. Blackwell. Botanica Caroliniana: Collaborative Research in the Liberal Arts. Furman University Department of Classics. Revision Date: 06-13-2013.

The images of selected specimens collected by George Frankin were photographed at the Sloane Herbarium, in the Natural History Museum, London, in June of 2013.
